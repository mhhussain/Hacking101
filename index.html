Types of hackers

  • White hat
  • Black hat
  • Grey hat

White hat

Professional security specialists, primarily working to prevent malicious hacking.

  • Antivirus
  • Code auditing
  • Encryption
  • Network and system admins

Black hat

Personal gain and malicious intent.

  • Stealing information -
    CC, SSN, medical records, etc.
  • Damage a system
  • Disturb the peace

Grey hat

Somewhere in the middle? Hacking for the sake of hacking, but not really helping or hindering anyone.
